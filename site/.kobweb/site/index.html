What We Do At Vidyadaan club of GVPCE(A)

A social club of GVPCE(A) which strives for providing better education to future generations. Our target audience is school children and we strive to improve their lives. We conduct various events in different domains like visiting schools and interacting with students and conducting competitions in poster making, writing, public speaking and so on.
